Resident Pastor : Pastor Webster Chipfupi

 

Webster Chipfupi is the resident Pastor of RFM Delft Assembly. Married to Rudo Ndiripo Chipfupi, they have two kids, a boy and a girl.

Pastor Webster Chipfupi is a young dynamic teacher of the Word passionate to see the body Christ walking in the full measure of God's blessing and power through Jesus Christ. Residing in Delft South and also a rising business man. RFM Delft Assembly is an ever growing church. They meet every week at Simunye Secondary School.

In Delft they strive to deliver the truth with excellence. They don't just preach love, but walk in love. They don't just preach faith but walk by faith. They don't just preach hope but hope in the Lord. These three Faith,Hope and Love are identified in them.
