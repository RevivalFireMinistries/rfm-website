Apostle T & S Doyce

 

Apostle T. Doyce is a dynamic minister of the gospel of Jesus Christ. Married to Sandra Doyce and blessed with 2 kids Athalia and Ben.

He has preached the gospel for more than 10 years in and around the globe. Popularly known as the ‘Voice of Africa’ , Apostle T. Doyce has been granted special grace when it comes to his understanding of the mystery surrounding the Kingdom of God. He holds mass crusades which have seen many lives being transformed.

He is the President of the ever-growing family of Revival Fire Ministries and also the Chancellor of New Breed On Fire School of Ministry. His ministry has since extended into the corporate world where he is fathering captains of industry globally who are in turn influencing the world of business with the gospel of the kingdom. This, he believes, is only the beginning of a mighty revolution that will see the whole earth being covered with the knowledge of the glory of God.
