Your Miracle Today

Do you want to be saved?

(Romans 10:13)

For whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved.
Being saved has nothing to do with your past, weaknesses or bad habits. It has everything to do with calling on the Name of the Lord.

If you confess with your mouth, "Jesus is Lord," and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved .(Romans 10:9) God loved the world and sent His only begotten Son to die for everyone's sins. Now all you have to do is to believe in what Jesus did. God wants you as you are. He doesn't want you to clean up your mess first then come to Him but He wants you as you are.
Salvation simply means God rescuing you from your sins and the power of the devil. If you believe and you want salvation, declare with your mouth the below prayer.
I Believe that Jesus is the Christ who came from heaven to earth in-order to save me. He died and God raised Him from the dead . I believe that He is Lord and today I declare that He is the Lord of my life . I receive eternal life and I am a new creation ,I am forgiven and I am now a child of God. Amen

Are you sick in body?

1 Peter 2:24 Who his own self bare our sins in his own body on the tree, that we, being dead to sins, should live unto righteousness: by whose stripes ye were healed.

The Bible clearly tells us that we were healed of all diseases by the stripes of Jesus. Note it says you are healed already, not trying to be healed. So if you are sick today, that sickness is not your portion. It is an error that needs to be fixed. And Jesus is the healer. He wants you healed and your duty is to accept the healing. As you study the Word, most of the people who Jesus met, He would ensure they were healed.

Large crowds came and brought many people who were paralyzed or blind or lame or unable to talk. They placed them, and many others, in front of Jesus, and he healed them all. (Matthew 15:30).

Today make up your mind to receive your healing in the name of Jesus.

Are you struggling financially?

(Deuteronomy 8:18)

But you shall remember the LORD your God, for it is he who gives you power to get wealth; that he may establish his covenant which he swore to your fathers, as at this day.

The book of Proverbs paints a rather vivid picture of how exactly God feels about the welfare of His people in regards to their pockets. Many people have a vague understanding of God's heart towards finances. They believe that God wants them to be poor.

God enjoys it when we are living in prosperity.

Psalms 35:27

"...Let the Lord be magnified, Who takes pleasure in the prosperity of His servant".

Why does God wants us to prosper
  • To meet our needs and take care of our families.
  • To help the poor.
  • To expand HIS Kingdom

Beloved, I wish above all things that you may prosper and be in health, even as your soul prospers. 3 John 2 It is God's desire that you as His son or daughter you be financially well off. So choose to trust in the Lord and your financial well being will change.

Marital problems?

Have you struggled to find happiness in your marriage?

Perhaps it is time you and your spouse invited God to direct your relationship. The road to a happy marriage takes great commitment, hard work and God's direction.

When was the last time you brought your spouse before the Lord in prayer and truly thanked God for your relationship? Struggling to find quality time together? How about praying with your partner and asking God how He would like you to use your time? Make a decision to commit your relationship to God and to put God and your partner first.

When you apply this,your marriage will be able to stand any storm. Not only that; you’ll also have plenty of fun together along the way!
