Brief History

Revival Fire Ministries was born as a result of a serious passion to network nations in Christ Jesus. Without any resources in a time of a serious economic meltdown in Harare Zimbabwe, the work began. A great revival that stretched for 2 years with services that transfromed a suburb called (Glen Norah A, Harare, Zimbambwe) and built a foundation for the church.

It became a silent practical school of ministry that saw the raising of current pillars in the ministry. In 2008 after understanding that it was our time to expand, we took a step into our second nation (South Africa). Now with branches in Harare, Pretoria, Strand, Stellenbosch, Nomzamo Kraiifontein, Eestierrivier, Dunoon and Delft.

Making use of hindsight, we believe we have just begun and the journey is going to be long.

We are taking it one step at a time and each step is a miracle.

Through our school of ministry (New Breed on Fire), we believe that we are raising sons who will go into the nations of the world and possess for Jesus.
