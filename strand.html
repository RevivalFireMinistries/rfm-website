Welcome to Strand

Resident Pastor : Pastor Julius Vutabwashe

 

Julius Vutabwashe a.k.a Pastor J, a teacher by calling, Pastoring the Strand Assembly. Married to Sikhulile Vutabwashe, blessed with 3 children.

Pastor J loves to search the simple and deeper mysteries hidden in the Word of God. He believes that in the word of God, there's more than what meets the eye, something in between the lines, a clear voice in the pages of the bible...that he is determined to find out. Pastor J is a practical teacher of the word, who desires to see the word come alive, bringing a change in people's lives, character, finances and even their approach to life.

His desire as a Teacher of the word, is to see a duplication of the grace of God upon his life into every one that enters their doors. They are witnessing God raising mighty man and women fulfilling God's call upon their lives. Whatever they do, they do with the next generation in mind. RFM Strand is situated in the Industrial area @ No 8 Boundary Way, Strand.
